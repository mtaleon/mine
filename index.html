How to Play

Goal

Reveal all cells without clicking on any mines.

Controls

  • Left-click to reveal a cell
  • Right-click to place/remove a flag
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that cell

Looking for deeper puzzles? Octile offers more complex logic challenges.
